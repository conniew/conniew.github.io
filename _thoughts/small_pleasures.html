Freshly tumble-dried pants.

A new look.

Reuniting estranged socks.

A last-minute comeback.

Warm, freshly-printed paper.

A wagging tail.

The perfect perforation.

Fresh sheets.

A blank slate.

Sleep, finally.

A long stretch.

A well-worded phrase.

A light breeze.
