Shadows, Plays, and Movies

Variation 1: Half the parts are filmed, the other half are live actors acting the missing parts in front of the screen.
Variation 2: Half the parts are actors on stage, the other half are silhouettes of actors behind a light screen.
Variation 3: Half the parts are filmed, the other half are shadow puppets from the projector.

Pixel Music

Sample a digital image and map pixel colors to notes. Search for images by keyword and play an orchestra of similar images.

Kite Writing

Long-exposure photography of colorful kites, taken from the ground on a day with blue skies and clouds.

Programming Music

Take a practical computer program, map notes to keywords, variables, or other syntax. Execute the code on various inputs to create music.

Play Acting

Variation 1: A simple solo piano piece where the majority of the notation is comments on how the pianist should act while playing a note.
Variation 2: A ballet solo where the ballerina occasionally dances to a piano and plays a couple notes. Perhaps an annoyed pianist is playing accompaniment.

Live Action Gaming

Street Fighter contestants taking orders from the audience.

Reverse Tourism, a compilation

Stand in or on tourist attractions. Take photos of other tourists.

Xylophone Stress Reliever

Install a giant xylophone in a padded room. Allow guests to bring in giant mallets and let out some musical feelings.

Ringtone Medley

An orchestral piece using popular ringtones as motifs.

Fight Song

Spar with musical swords that resonate in different tones depending on the type of swing.
